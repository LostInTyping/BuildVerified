## Overview

Ohio Sentencing Data Platform is a web ecosystem for collecting sentencing data in a comprehensive, searchable database. It is designed to tell the story of sentencing in Ohio, inform decision-making, improve public and practitioner access to data, and support standard sentencing entry workflows.

## Client

Ohio Criminal Sentencing Commission | Supreme Court of Ohio

## Technologies Used

React, Node.js, Express, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, TypeScript

## Design and Architecture

Domain-Driven Design, role-based access control, E2E testing, unit testing, monorepo
